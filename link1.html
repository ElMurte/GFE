    How do I remove someone who is using my account without my permission?

    To remove unauthorized access to your NetMovies account, we recommend that you sign out of all devices on your account by visiting Manage Devices on the Account page under Settings. Please note, deactivating devices may take up to 8 hours to take effect. After the devices have been removed, we recommend that you change your password. You may also wish to review our recommendations for keeping your Netflix account and personal information safe.

    How do I hide titles from my viewing history?

    We give you the ability to hide titles from your viewing history from your active Netmovies account.
